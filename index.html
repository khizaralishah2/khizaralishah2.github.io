Just for you, Aainy

You carry a name that means light of the eye — and that is exactly what you are to me. Every room you walk into becomes softer. Every silence you fill becomes music. You are not just the person I love; you are the reason I understand what love is supposed to feel like.

I promise to spend every day making sure you know this. Not just today, but on the quiet mornings, the long evenings, and all the ordinary days in between.

You are my Aainy. My home. My always.

forever & always
